About Us

Learn more about our journey, our team, and our commitment to wellness

Our Story

Founded in 2010, SerenitySpas began with a simple vision: to create a sanctuary where people could escape the stresses of everyday life and find true relaxation. Our founder, Emma Thompson, had spent years working in the wellness industry and saw a need for a spa that combined traditional techniques with modern innovations.

Starting with just three treatment rooms and a small team of dedicated therapists, we quickly gained a reputation for exceptional service and results-driven treatments. As word spread, we expanded our facilities and service offerings to meet the growing demand.

Today, SerenitySpas has grown into a premier wellness destination with multiple locations, but our core mission remains unchanged: to provide each client with a personalized experience that promotes balance, rejuvenation, and overall well-being.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at SerenitySpas

Personalized Care

We believe that every client is unique, with individual needs and preferences. Our treatments are customized to address your specific concerns and goals, ensuring optimal results and satisfaction.

Natural & Sustainable

We are committed to using high-quality, natural products that are kind to your skin and to the environment. Our eco-friendly practices extend to all aspects of our operations.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our treatments to the cleanliness of our facilities. Our team undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of industry developments.
