Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies

General Questions

Our main spa is located at 123 Relaxation Blvd, Serenity City, SC 12345. We have ample parking available for our guests. You can find detailed directions on our Contact page or give us a call at (555) 123-4567 for assistance.

Yes, we offer gift certificates for all of our services and packages. They can be purchased in-person at our spa, over the phone, or through our website. Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase and make the perfect gift for any occasion.

Services Questions

We offer a variety of massage therapies including Swedish, Deep Tissue, Hot Stone, Aromatherapy, Sports Massage, Prenatal Massage, and Couples Massage. Each type targets different needs, from relaxation to pain relief. Our therapists can help you choose the best option based on your specific concerns.

Our treatments range from 30 minutes to 2 hours, depending on the service. Massages are typically available in 60, 75, or 90-minute sessions. Facials usually last 60 minutes, while body treatments can range from 45 to 90 minutes. Spa packages may last several hours and include multiple services.

We use high-quality, natural products in all of our treatments. Our skincare line is free from harsh chemicals, parabens, and artificial fragrances. We prioritize organic and sustainable ingredients whenever possible. Many of the products used in our treatments are also available for purchase, allowing you to continue your skincare routine at home.

Absolutely! All of our services are suitable for both men and women. We offer specialized men's facials that address specific skincare concerns like razor burn and ingrown hairs. Our massage therapies are equally beneficial for everyone, and we have male therapists available upon request.

Booking Questions

You can book an appointment through our website, by calling us at (555) 123-4567, or in person at our spa. Online booking is available 24/7 and allows you to see real-time availability. We recommend booking at least a week in advance for weekends and popular times, though we do accommodate same-day appointments when possible.

We recommend booking 1-2 weeks in advance for regular appointments, and 3-4 weeks in advance for weekend appointments or special occasions. During holiday seasons, it's best to book even earlier. For spa packages or group bookings, we suggest booking at least one month ahead to ensure availability.

Yes, you can request a specific therapist when booking your appointment. If you've had a positive experience with a particular staff member, we're happy to schedule you with them again. If your preferred therapist is not available at your desired time, we can suggest another qualified professional or offer alternative appointment times.

Yes, we welcome group bookings for special occasions such as bridal parties, corporate events, or birthday celebrations. We offer special packages for groups and can customize services to suit your needs. For groups of 4 or more, please contact our spa coordinator directly at (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 to arrange your visit.

Policies Questions

We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows will be charged the full service amount. We understand that emergencies happen, and we handle these situations on a case-by-case basis.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), cash, and gift certificates. We also accept digital payment methods like Apple Pay and Google Pay. Payment is required at the time of service. For group bookings or spa packages, a deposit may be required at the time of booking.

Gratuities are not included in the service price and are left to your discretion based on your satisfaction with the service. The industry standard is 15-20% of the service price. For your convenience, you can add a gratuity when paying by credit card, or you can provide cash directly to your service provider.

If you're not completely satisfied with your service, please let us know immediately so we can address your concerns. We may offer to redo the service or provide a credit toward a future visit. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis and must be requested within 7 days of service. Gift certificates are non-refundable but are transferable.
