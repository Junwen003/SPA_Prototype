About SerenitySpas

Founded in 2010, SerenitySpas has been dedicated to providing exceptional spa experiences that promote wellness and relaxation. Our team of certified therapists and estheticians are committed to helping you achieve balance in body and mind.

We use only premium, natural products and the latest techniques to ensure that every treatment delivers the results you desire. Our peaceful environment is designed to help you escape the stresses of everyday life.
